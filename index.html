About ME

My name is Kevin El-Saikali, and I am a second-year Software Engineering student at the University of Ottawa.

I have a strong interest in web development and enjoy creating user-friendly applications. I am always eager to find new ways to learn and to improve my dev skills :)

My Work Ethic...

As a UI/UX designer, I approach my work by putting the user at the center of every decision. I start by researching and understanding their needs, goals, and frustrations so I can design experiences that are both intuitive and meaningful. I prioritize usability and accessibility, making sure that what I create is not only visually appealing but also functional for everyone.

Collaboration is key for me—I work closely with developers and fellow designers to ensure alignment across the project. I believe in constant iteration, using prototypes, and user feedback to refine my designs. I stay curious, keep up with design trends, and always focus on solving real problems through thoughtful, user-centered design.
